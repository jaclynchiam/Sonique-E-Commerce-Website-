OVERVIEW

Product Description

The Sonique WF-C510 are budget-friendly, truly wireless earbuds that offer clear, well-balanced sound with support for features like 360 Reality Audio, transparency mode, and multipoint Bluetooth pairing. They have a lightweight, comfortable design, are sweat-resistant (IPX4), and boast a long battery life of up to 22 hours with the charging case. While they lack active noise cancellation and wireless charging, they make up for it with solid audio performance, easy controls, and great overall value for casual, everyday listening.

Product Features

The Sonique WF-C510 earbuds feature high-quality sound with 6mm drivers and DSEE technology that enhances compressed audio, along with support for immersive 360 Reality Audio. They offer stable Bluetooth 5.3 connectivity with multipoint pairing, allowing connection to two devices simultaneously. With up to 11 hours of battery life from the earbuds and an additional 11 hours from the charging case, they provide a total of 22 hours of playback, plus a quick charge function that gives 1 hour of use from just 5 minutes of charging. Designed for comfort and portability, each earbud weighs only 4.6g and features an ergonomic fit. They are IPX4-rated for water and sweat resistance, making them ideal for workouts or daily use. The earbuds include customizable physical button controls and charge via a convenient USB-C port.

Specification

The Sonique WF-C510 earbuds come equipped with 6mm dynamic drivers and support for DSEE (Digital Sound Enhancement Engine), enhancing the clarity of compressed audio files. They use Bluetooth 5.3 for a stable wireless connection and support the AAC audio codec, with multipoint pairing that allows connection to two devices at the same time. The earbuds offer a battery life of up to 11 hours, with an additional 11 hours provided by the charging case, totaling 22 hours of playback. A quick charge feature gives 1 hour of use from just 5 minutes of charging. Each earbud weighs approximately 4.6 grams, and the set is IPX4-rated, making it resistant to sweat and light splashes. Charging is done via a USB-C port, and the earbuds also include customizable physical button controls for music, volume, and calls.
