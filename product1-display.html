OVERVIEW

Product Description

The Sonique WH-CH720 is a premium pair of wireless noise-cancelling headphones known for its industry-leading ANC (Active Noise Cancellation), excellent sound quality, and long-lasting comfort. Equipped with Sony is advanced QN1 noise-cancelling processor, these headphones effectively block out ambient noise, making them ideal for travel, work, or daily use. The audio is rich and immersive, with support for high-resolution formats and real-time upscaling via DSEE Extreme. With up to 30 hours of battery life and a quick charge feature, users can enjoy uninterrupted listening throughout the day. Additional smart features like touch controls, speak-to-chat, and Bluetooth multipoint connection enhance convenience, while the comfortable, lightweight design ensures a great fit for extended wear.

Product Features

The Sonique WH-CH720 features powerful noise cancellation, up to 30 hours of battery life, and premium sound with LDAC and DSEE Extreme. It includes smart features like touch controls, speak-to-chat, wear detection, and Bluetooth multipoint for easy device switching.

Specification

The Sonique WH-CH720is a wireless over-ear headphone weighing approximately 254 grams, featuring 40mm drivers and a frequency response of 4Hz to 40kHz. It supports Bluetooth 5.0 with codecs including SBC, AAC, and LDAC for high-quality wireless audio. The battery lasts up to 30 hours with active noise cancellation on, and a quick 10-minute charge provides up to 5 hours of playback. It includes a USB-C charging port, touch sensor controls, and built-in microphones for voice calls and smart assistant access.
