All prices are listed and may change at any time without prior notice. In case of any pricing errors or display mistakes, we reserve the right to correct them and take necessary action, such as cancelling an order if needed.

Orders

Once an order is placed, customers will receive a confirmation email. However, orders may be cancelled by us if:

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How Long Is The Delivery Time?

Delivery typically takes 3–7 working days, depending on your location and courier availability.

2. Can I Return My Product If Faulty?

Yes, if your item is faulty or damaged upon arrival, you can request a return within 7 days. Please email us at support@sonique.com with your order details and photos.

3. Do You Accept Change-Of-Mind Returns?

Sorry, we currently do not accept returns for change of mind. Please double-check your order before confirming.

4. Is There A Warranty For Sonique Products?

Yes, our products come with a 30-day limited warranty for manufacturing defects. Warranty does not cover physical damage or misuse.

5. What Payment Methods Are Accepted?

We accept online banking, credit/debit cards, and other payment gateways (based on the system you display on your website).
