OVERVIEW

Product Description

The Sonique LinkBuds S are wireless sport headphones designed for all-day comfort and active lifestyles. With a secure fit and sweat resistance, they are ideal for intense workouts or long listening sessions.

Product Features

The Sonique LinkBuds S offer active noise cancellation powered by the Sonique Integrated Processor V1, along with LDAC and DSEE Extreme for high-resolution audio. Featuring adaptive sound control, the earbuds automatically switch between ambient sound and noise cancellation based on your activity. Weighing just 4.8 grams per earbud, they ensure all-day comfort with a compact and ergonomic design. The earbuds deliver up to 6 hours of playback with ANC on, or 9 hours without, plus 14 additional hours with the charging case. A quick 5-minute charge gives you 1 hour of playback. Other highlights include Precise Voice Pickup technology for crystal-clear calls, IPX4 water resistance, Bluetooth 5.2, Google Fast Pair, Windows Swift Pair, and smart features such as Speak-to-Chat.

Specification

The Sonique LinkBuds S are powered by 5mm dynamic drivers and the Sonique Integrated Processor V1 for superior noise cancellation performance. They support Bluetooth 5.2 and are compatible with LDAC, AAC, and SBC audio codecs. Each earbud weighs approximately 4.8 grams and is rated IPX4 for water and sweat resistance. With active noise cancellation turned on, the earbuds offer up to 6 hours of battery life, extending to 9 hours when ANC is off. The charging case provides an additional 14 hours. A 5-minute quick charge offers up to 1 hour of playback. These earbuds also include features like Precise Voice Pickup, adaptive sound control, Speak-to-Chat, and fast pairing capabilities with Android and Windows platforms.
