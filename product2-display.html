OVERVIEW

Product Description

The Sonique WH-CH520 offers impressive 50-hour battery life, Bluetooth 5.2, and customizable sound via the Sony Headphones Connect app. Lightweight and comfortable, they support multipoint connection, DSEE audio enhancement, and Fast/Swift Pairing. While they lack noise cancellation and have a plastic build, they deliver great value for casual listening with clear, balanced sound and excellent wireless performance—all at an affordable price. Best for: Daily commuters, students, and budget-focused users who want long battery life and decent sound.

Product Features

TThe Sonique WH-CH520 features up to 50 hours of battery life with fast charging, a lightweight and comfortable on-ear design, Bluetooth 5.2 with multipoint pairing, DSEE audio enhancement for clearer sound, customizable EQ via the Sony Headphones Connect app, a built-in microphone for hands-free calls, and quick pairing with Android and Windows devices using Fast Pair and Swift Pair.

Specification

The Sonique WH-CH520 is a lightweight on-ear Bluetooth 5.2 headphone with 30mm drivers, up to 50 hours of battery life, USB-C fast charging, DSEE audio enhancement, multipoint pairing, built-in mic, and customizable EQ via the Sony Headphones Connect app.
