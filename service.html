2. Product Setup & Troubleshooting

Need help setting up or fixing your Sonique device? Here are some quick solutions to common issues:

How to Pair via Bluetooth:

  1. Turn on your Sonique headphones or earbuds.
  2. Enable Bluetooth on your device (phone, tablet, etc.).
  3. Select “Sonique” from the list of available devices.
  4. Wait for the “Connected” notification.

One Side Not Working:

  • Make sure both earbuds are fully charged.
  • Place both earbuds in the case and take them out again.
  • Try resetting the device (see below).

How to Reset Your Sonique Device:

  1. Place both earbuds back into the charging case.
  2. Press and hold the reset button (or both touch sensors) for 10 seconds.
  3. Remove from the case and reconnect via Bluetooth.

If issues still happen, contact us at support@sonique.com for further assistance.

3. Warranty Claims Guide

We offer a 30-day limited warranty for all Sonique products.

Covered under warranty:

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Product malfunction not caused by misuse

How to submit a claim:

  1. Email us at support@sonique.com
  2. Attach a clear photo or video of the issue
  3. Include your order number and proof of purchase

We aim to respond within 1–3 working days.

4. Track Your Order

To check your delivery status:

  1. Find your tracking number in the order confirmation email
  2. Visit the courier's tracking website and enter the tracking number
  3. If there are no updates after 3 days, please contact us
